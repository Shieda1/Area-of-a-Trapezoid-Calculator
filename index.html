What is the area of a trapezoid calculator?
The area of a trapezoid is the number of unit squares that can be fit into the trapezoid and it is calculated by multiplying the average of the bases by the altitude.
How to calculate area of a trapezoid calculator?
To calculate area of a trapezoid calculator you should use the following formula:
A = ((a + b) / 2) * h
where:
a = long base
b = short base
h = altitude
Why use area of a trapezoid calculator?
The area of a trapezoid calculator is used to find the region enclosed by a trapezoid.
